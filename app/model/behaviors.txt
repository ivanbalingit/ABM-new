Behavior Rules:
- Turtles (people) move around randomly according to the set probability of moving.

-- If an infected turtle (red) approaches near an uninfected and non-vaccinated turtle (blue), 
-- the uninfected turtle will become infected (red). 

-- If an infected turtle (red) approaches near an uninfected and vaccinated turtle (green),
-- the uninfected turtle will remain uninfected.
